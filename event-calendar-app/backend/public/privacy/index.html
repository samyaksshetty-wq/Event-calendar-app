Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Namma Events ("the app") is a local events calendar. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

Information we collect

The app does not require you to create an account, and does not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers to browse events.

  • Recent searches — the terms you search for are saved on your own device only, so you can quickly re-run a past search. This never leaves your phone and is not sent to our servers.
  • Event data — event listings (titles, dates, venues, descriptions, brochures) are added by the app's administrator, not submitted by app users.

If you contact us to list an event

If you reach out to have your event featured (by phone or email), we use the contact details you give us solely to communicate about that listing. This contact happens outside the app itself and is not collected through the app.

Third-party services

The app is built using the following infrastructure providers, who process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — hosts the event database and any images/brochures/ads shown in the app.
  • Render — hosts the backend server that the app communicates with.

Neither service is used to track individual app users or serve personalized/third-party advertising.

Advertising

Any ads shown in the app are sold and managed directly by us — we do not use third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob), and no ad-tracking or user-profiling takes place through these ads.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top.
